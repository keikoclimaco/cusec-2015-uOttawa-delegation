About

CUSEC 2015 uOttawa Delegation

What is CUSEC?

The Canadian University Software Engineering Conference is an annual event held in Montreal since 2002. It is a non-profit conference organized by the students for the students. CUSEC brings students from across the country together to learn, network and talk about software engineering and computer science.

The uOttawa Delegation

This year, my goal as Head Delegate is to bring THE LARGEST EVER uOttawa Delegation to CUSEC 2015 at a reasonable price. I also hope to encourage the members of our delegation to participate in CUSEC events and show other schools what uOttawa is made of.

Visit this page often to get the latest news, updates and information about the uOttawa Delegation: cost, registration, funding grants and more!

Services

What is it exactly that I can do for you?

Ticket

I can take care of reserving your ticket to CUSEC.

Accommodation

I can take care of reserving your hotel room and making sure you are roomies with the friends you requested.

Transportation

I can take care of getting you to and from Montreal. (Nah, it won't be a plane... I just couldn't find a bus icon.)

Funding

I will do my best to find funding, grants and sponsorships to give you as large of a refund as possible.

Wow, so awesome right? So what do you have to do?
  1. Simply fill out the Google Docs Form (I will be posting that when the registration period starts)
  2. Bring your payment to SITE 4026 during our office hours.
      Please remember:
    • We accept cash or cheque only. Please issue cheques to the IEEE uOttawa Student Branch.
    • IEEE office hours schedule can be found here.
  3. Make sure you grab a copy of your receipt! You will receive a confirmation email verifying that I have received your payment and your spot is reserved. (Will take up to 24 hours).
  4. Bring your beautiful and awesome self to CUSEC in January!
  5. Wait patiently for your reimbursement cheque.
**Please note that due to the great number of students who have expressed interest in going to CUSEC this year, your spot is NOT RESERVED until your payment is RECEIVED.**
You will learn more at CUSEC in 3 days than your entire 1st year of university
Michel Parisien, Student, Concordia University
