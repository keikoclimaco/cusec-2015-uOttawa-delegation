Hi there!

My name is Keiko Climaco and I am your uOttawa Head Delegate to CUSEC 2015. I've set this page up to update the students about any necessary information about my goals, duties and activities as HD this year.

This page also hopes to acknowledge any professors, institutions and organizations that will lend us a helping hand to enable students to go to CUSEC.

This year, my main goal is to achieve the LARGEST EVER uOttawa Delegation to CUSEC 2015. 35 out of the 49 students from last year's delegation are still students. Therefore, I should have absolutely no problem with that goal. However, I also hope to achieve that feat with every student going to CUSEC at a reasonable price. I will be working hard to find methods to subsidize costs for the entire delegation. If you would like to make a contribution, or know of any possible methods of sponsorship and funding: please let me know! :D

I also hope to encourage younger students to start going to CUSEC early. I think that it's important to realize early, the amount of tools and knowledge there is out there to become successful developers in the future.

I am aiming to encourage a higher participation rate of uOttawa students in CUSEC's events like DemoCamp and Resume Roast. By providing an incentive, I hope that the all members of our delegation will be encouraged to show other schools what uOttawa engineers, computer scientists and enthusiasts are made of.

If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact me!

Keiko Climaco
